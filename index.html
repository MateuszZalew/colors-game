Colors roulette

Game rules

You start with 200$. When you place a bet and click Spin!, 3 rectangles gain a color from the pool (red, green or blue). You win 2x the amount you bet if all the rectangles are of the same color(e.g. all rectangles are blue) or each rectangle has a different color (e.g. 1 blue, 1 red and 1 green). You lose in every other case therefore losing the amount you bet. Good luck!
